Life is like a flowing river!

Life is like a long flowing river,
For some it's century miles
While for some a less than that
We need to learn to swim like a fish
Always against the flow
but you need to be a lot more than a fish
Weathers are not the same always
Swim above the water and swim under it
Long breaths are much required
When the storm is alive
You need to find the shore
And wait for the right time
Patience will calm the water
And then you can swim again.
Sometimes to go for a long journey
You'll need to build a boat
It is much necessary that
You learn to survive
In the storms, floods, and the rains
In life under it and above it.
You need to learn to take long breaths
Silence will guide you,
Chaos in your mind will drown you
But here patience is the key
To live and survive in the life like a river.
