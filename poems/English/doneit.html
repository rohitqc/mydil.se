The Walk- I have done it...

Miles of smiles
Walking restless
Talking to strangers
I have done it...
From busy life
Walk of mindfulness
A bit of for myself
I have done it...
Yes it was hard
It was rough
Through meadows
Through puddles
Through mountains
Crossing rivers
I have done it ...
Through the rough weather,
Winds blowing at 75miles and hour
Through the rain showers
And cold breeze of 1 degree Celsius
Passing all the hurdles
The long memorable walk of 6 hours
I have done it...
— Rohit
