I'll be there...

I'll be there with you...!
with every step you take,
with every breathe you inhale,
with every blink of your eye,
with every beat of your heart,
I will always be there with you...!
with every happy moment you live
with every sad moment you might suffer
with every passing cloud of joy or bitterness
I will always be there there for you...!
I'll be there...
waiting for you,
looking at horizon,
fading sun, dimming light, when it's getting darker,
shining like the moon light,to guide you,
I am here waiting for you,
I am here remembering only you,
Untill the end of my life!, I'll be here just for you and only you...
— Rohit
